Inception
Actors Quotes

Inception is a science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. The story revolves around a skilled thief, Dom Cobb, who steals secrets from within the subconscious during the dream state. He is given a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can implant another person's idea into a target's subconscious.

Actors

  • Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb
  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
  • Elliot Page as Ariadne
  • Tom Hardy as Eames
  • Ken Watanabe as Saito
  • Marion Cotillard as Mal Cobb
